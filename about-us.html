Who The Fuck Is WoBBoB??

From grafting on the streets in The South West and Wiltshire, rolling with hardened criminals in and around the Brittish Capital, to smashing into Drum & Bass DJing and Production and AI development, WoBBoB's journey is one that not one soul would wish to endure, self-belief, and triumph against the odds. It’s a story that inspires, a narrative filled with struggle and redemption, proving that determination can turn the darkest chapters into a light that shines brightly for others to follow.

He faced addiction, crime, and undiagnosed mental health challenges—but never gave in. A builder by trade, WoBBoB forged his own path, self-taught in music production and later coding, launching two massive projects: BoBBoT and the Trade 'A' Them Bot.

His tracks like Wide Asleep and Quantum Bass aren't just bangers—they’re his truth in sound. Every beat, every drop, tells part of his journey.

Now with near-complete AI projects under his belt, WoBBoB's vision is bigger than ever: to inspire, to build, and to lead by example. Against all odds, he’s done it solo—and he’s just getting started.
