About Me

I'm WoBBoB—a name forged from the depths of adversity and the heights of creativity. My journey through life's challenges has been the crucible in which my passion for drum and bass was ignited. As a producer and DJ, I channel my experiences into every beat, creating soundscapes that resonate with authenticity and raw energy.

My SoundCloud page showcases a collection of mixes that reflect my evolution as an artist. Tracks like "Missy Elliott Vs Eve Blendahh," "Wide Asleep," "Hitch Higher," and "Fast Perspectives" are testaments to my dedication to the craft and my desire to push the boundaries of the genre. Notably, my debut remix, "Your Mind -is- Tap Ho," in collaboration with AMC, Formula, and TC, marked a significant milestone in my musical journey.

But my pursuits don't end with music. I've ventured into the realm of algorithmic trading, developing bots like Trade'A'Them Bot and Bob Bot. These creations are the result of countless hours of coding and a relentless drive to innovate. They represent my commitment to excellence, whether on the dance floor or in the digital marketplace.

Everything I do is for my children—the heartbeat behind every track, the motivation behind every line of code. They are my inspiration, my purpose, and the reason I strive for greatness in all my endeavors.

This isn't just a career; it's a calling. Welcome to the world of WoBBoB.
