WoBBoB: A Journey of Resilience, Reinvention, and Innovation

From Drum & Bass to AI – The story of passion, struggle, and success.

The Early Battles

From the streets of South West and Wiltshire, WoBBoB faced adversity head-on. Overcoming struggles with addiction, crime, and mental health challenges, he found a path of self-improvement through music and technology.

A Self-Made Path

Starting as a builder, he transitioned into music production and later taught himself coding. With no formal training, WoBBoB mastered the skills needed to create groundbreaking AI and music projects.

Drum & Bass Achievements

WoBBoB's music reflects his life—intense, raw, and full of energy. His tracks like Wide Asleep and Quantum Bass have gained recognition in the Drum & Bass scene.
